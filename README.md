# QR Generator

An open-source QR generator with customization. Feel at ease with no marketing trackers or pay walls.

## Features

1. Basic no-themed QR generation
2. Customization of the colors of the QR
3. Adding theme to the QR 
4. Download or save the generated QR

## Dependencies

[node-qrcode library](https://github.com/soldair/node-qrcode).
[merge-images](https://www.npmjs.com/package/merge-images?activeTab=readme)
